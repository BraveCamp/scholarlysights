~Field Trip Ideas

Museums, Parks, and nature areas are really great places to bring students on a field trip. One of the biggest reasons we sugjest field trips is because students need to get out of the classrooms or classroom in school. Students need to move around and take a break from being stuffed with knowledge. Below we have put various ideas for schools in chicago to go on field trips

  • Lincoln Park Zoo
